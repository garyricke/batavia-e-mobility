About the project

Our school is participating in the Roll Smart, Ride Safe video campaign — a fun, fast-paced series of short videos that teach students about bike & e-bike safety, helmet use, and traffic laws.

What participation looks like

  • Lead roles: delivering short scripted safety lines (e.g., "Yikes! Glad I wore my helmet!").
  • Light acting: dusting off after a simulated near-miss, picking up a bike, reacting to a close call.
  • Extras: reacting in the background to show surprise or relief.
  • Wardrobe: normal riding clothes — bring your own bike helmet.
Safety: All "crashes" and "near-misses" are simulated through acting and creative editing. No students will be placed in actual physical danger during filming.
